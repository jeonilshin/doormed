Cholesterol Control (Statins & Others)
Statins (First-line): Atorvastatin (Lipitor), Rosuvastatin (Crestor), Simvastatin (Zocor), Pravastatin (Pravachol), Lovastatin.
Non-Statin/Combination: Ezetimibe (Zetia), Bempedoic acid (Nexletol), PCSK9 inhibitors (Repatha, Praluent). 
Blood Thinners (Anticoagulants & Antiplatelets) 
Anticoagulants (for AFib/clot prevention): Apixaban (Eliquis), Rivaroxaban (Xarelto), Dabigatran (Pradaxa), Warfarin (Coumadin).
Antiplatelets (for heart attack/stroke prevention): Aspirin, Clopidogrel (Plavix), Ticagrelor (Brilinta).

Antihistamines (Allergies) 
Non-Drowsy (Second Generation): Cetirizine (Zyrtec), Loratadine (Claritin), Fexofenadine (Allegra), Desloratadine (Clarinex).
Drowsy/First Generation: Diphenhydramine (Benadryl), Chlorpheniramine.
